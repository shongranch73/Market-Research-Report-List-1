<p><p>The electro-optical converters market encompasses various types, including fiber optic, digital output, analog output, and others. Fiber optic converters facilitate high-speed data transmission over light signals, ideal for long-distance communication. Digital output converters translate optical signals into digital data suited for electronic devices. Analog output converters convert signals into a continuous waveform for applications requiring precise signal representation. Other types may include hybrid or specialized converters designed for niche markets, enhancing the versatility and functionality of optical communication systems.</p></p>
