<p><p>The electromagnetic viscometer market is divided into two primary types: laboratory viscometers and process viscometers. Laboratory viscometers are designed for precise measurement of fluid viscosity in controlled environments, often used for research and quality control. In contrast, process viscometers are integrated into production lines to continuously monitor and control viscosity during manufacturing, ensuring consistent product quality. Both types leverage electromagnetic principles to provide accurate and reliable viscosity measurements across various industries, including chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
