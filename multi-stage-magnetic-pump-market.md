<p><strong>The Multi-stage Magnetic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-stage magnetic pump market features two primary types: stationary shaft and rotating shaft pumps. Stationary shaft pumps utilize a fixed shaft with magnetic couplings, providing a seamless design ideal for high-efficiency applications. In contrast, rotating shaft pumps feature a dynamic shaft that rotates in conjunction with the magnetic drive, allowing for increased fluid flow and versatility. Both types leverage magnetic technology to eliminate mechanical seals, enhancing reliability and minimizing maintenance in various industries, including chemical processing and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
