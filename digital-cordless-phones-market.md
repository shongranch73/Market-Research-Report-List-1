<p><p>The digital cordless phone market is primarily categorized into three frequency bands: 2.4 GHz, 5.8 GHz, and DECT 6.0. The 2.4 GHz band is commonly used but may experience interference from other devices like Wi-Fi. The 5.8 GHz band offers better clarity and reduced interference, making it suitable for areas with dense electronic use. DECT 6.0 is designed for secure communication and superior range, operating on a frequency specifically allocated for cordless phones, ensuring minimal interference and enhanced audio quality.</p></p>
