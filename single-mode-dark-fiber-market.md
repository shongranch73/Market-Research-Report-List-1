<p><p>The single mode dark fiber market encompasses two key types: glass and plastic. Glass fiber is the predominant choice, offering superior performance for long-distance communication due to its lower attenuation and higher bandwidth capabilities. It is commonly used in telecommunications and high-speed internet infrastructure. Conversely, plastic fiber provides a cost-effective alternative suitable for shorter distances and less demanding applications. While glass fiber is ideal for critical, high-capacity networks, plastic fiber is increasingly favored for residential and localized connections.</p></p>
