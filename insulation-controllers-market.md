<p><p>Insulation controllers are essential components in electrical systems, categorized into three main types: suspension, line post, and braced line post. Suspension insulators are used to support overhead power lines, providing flexibility and stability. Line post insulators are vertically oriented, typically mounted on poles to secure wires effectively in substations or along distribution lines. Braced line post insulators combine the features of both, offering enhanced support and stability for medium to high-voltage applications, ensuring safe and efficient energy transmission.</p></p>
