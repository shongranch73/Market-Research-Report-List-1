<p><p>The Laser Annealer market comprises two primary types: Line Beam Type and Multi Lens Array Type. The Line Beam Type utilizes a single, continuous laser beam to uniformly heat the substrate, ensuring efficient processing for large areas. In contrast, the Multi Lens Array Type employs multiple lenses to generate several beams, allowing for simultaneous annealing across diverse regions, enhancing throughput and precision. Both types cater to different applications in semiconductor manufacturing, improving material properties and device performance.</p></p>
