<p><p>Low Temperature Cofired Ceramics (LTCC) materials are categorized into two primary types: High-Frequency and Regular Types. High-Frequency LTCCs are designed for microwave and RF applications, offering low dielectric losses and high insulation resistance, making them ideal for telecommunications and satellite communications. Regular LTCCs, while still versatile, are primarily used in electronic devices requiring standard performance levels for circuit integration, such as sensors and power electronics. Both types facilitate miniaturization and improved performance in various electronic applications.</p></p>
