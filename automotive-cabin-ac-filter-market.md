<p><p>The automotive cabin AC filter market includes three main types: electrostatic filters, particulate filters, and charcoal filters. Electrostatic filters use static electricity to attract and capture dust and allergens, enhancing air quality. Particulate filters efficiently trap particles like pollen and dust, providing a clean air environment. Charcoal filters contain activated carbon that absorbs odors and harmful gases, improving cabin air freshness. Each filter type plays a crucial role in ensuring a healthy driving experience by maintaining optimal air quality inside vehicles.</p></p>
