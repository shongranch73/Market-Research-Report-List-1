<p><p>Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) equipment encompasses several market types. Metal ALD specializes in depositing metallic films with high precision for applications like semiconductor manufacturing. Aluminum Oxide ALD focuses on creating thin aluminum oxide layers, ideal for electronics and insulation. ALD on Polymers enables coating of various polymer substrates, enhancing their properties. Catalytic ALD is tailored for catalysts, facilitating the creation of active layers on substrates, thus improving efficiency in chemical processes. Each type addresses specific industry needs for advanced material fabrication.</p></p>
