<p><p>The antibody drugs market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Monoclonal Antibodies (mAbs)** are engineered to target specific antigens, primarily used for treating cancers and autoimmune diseases.</p><p>2. **Antibody-Drug Conjugates (ADCs)** combine monoclonal antibodies with cytotoxic drugs, allowing targeted delivery of chemotherapy to cancer cells, minimizing damage to healthy tissue.</p><p>3. **Polyclonal Antibodies** are derived from multiple immune cell clones, used primarily for research and diagnostic applications due to their ability to recognize various epitopes on an antigen.</p></p>
