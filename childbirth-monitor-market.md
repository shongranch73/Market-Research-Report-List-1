<p><strong>The Childbirth Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bedside Monitor</li><li>Central Monitor</li><li>Discharge Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The childbirth monitor market encompasses three primary types: bedside monitors, central monitors, and discharge monitors. Bedside monitors provide real-time monitoring of maternal and fetal vital signs during labor, ensuring immediate responses to any complications. Central monitors aggregate data from multiple bedside units, allowing healthcare providers to monitor several patients simultaneously from a central location, enhancing efficiency and oversight. Discharge monitors assist in tracking mothers and newborns after delivery, ensuring timely follow-ups and minimizing complications during the postnatal period.</p></p>
