<p><strong>The Automotive Camera Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS)</li><li>Charge-Coupled Device (CCD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive camera sensor market primarily features two types: Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) and Charge-Coupled Device (CCD) sensors. CMOS sensors are more prevalent due to their lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and ability to integrate additional functions on a single chip, making them ideal for advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous vehicles. CCD sensors, known for their superior image quality and sensitivity, are less commonly used in automotive applications, primarily due to higher costs and power requirements.</p></p>
