<p><p>LoRaWAN modules operate across various frequency bands, each suited for specific regional regulations and applications. The 433MHz and 470MHz bands are commonly used in Asia for short-range communications. The 868MHz is prevalent in Europe, while the 915MHz band is popular in North America for IoT applications. The 923MHz band is often utilized in Southeast Asia. These variations allow for tailored solutions depending on regional requirements, transmission range, and interference levels in different environments, enhancing the versatility of LoRa technology.</p></p>
