<p><p>The SMT-SPI (Solder Paste Inspection) system market is categorized into in-line and off-line systems. In-line SPI systems are integrated into the production line, allowing for real-time inspection of solder paste application, enhancing efficiency and minimizing defects during the manufacturing process. Off-line SPI systems, on the other hand, operate separately from the production line, enabling detailed inspections and quality control checks after the solder paste is applied. Both systems play crucial roles in ensuring the quality and reliability of electronic assemblies.</p></p>
