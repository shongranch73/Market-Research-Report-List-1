<p><p>The high-pressure waterjet cutting machine market comprises two primary types: cantilever and gantry systems. In contrast, gantry systems utilize a dual-rail framework, providing stability and the ability to handle larger, heavy materials with high cutting precision over expansive areas. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing manufacturing efficiency through advanced cutting technologies.</p></p>
