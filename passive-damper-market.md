<p><p>The passive damper market consists of hydraulic and pneumatic types, each serving to reduce vibrations and enhance stability. Hydraulic dampers utilize fluid dynamics to absorb energy and provide resistance, making them suitable for heavy machinery and automotive applications. In contrast, pneumatic dampers rely on compressed air to achieve similar effects, often used in lighter applications like industrial equipment and consumer products. Both types are essential in improving performance and safety in various sectors, including construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
